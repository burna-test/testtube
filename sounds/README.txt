Please add the following sound files to this folder:

1. engine.mp3 - A looping engine sound that works well when the playback rate is adjusted
2. wind.mp3 - A looping wind/air rushing sound
3. takeoff.mp3 - A short sound effect for when the plane takes off
4. landing.mp3 - A short sound effect for when the plane lands

You can find free sound effects at websites like:
- Freesound.org (https://freesound.org/)
- Pixabay Sound Effects (https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/)
- SoundBible (https://soundbible.com/)
- ZapSplat (https://www.zapsplat.com/)

Look for airplane or engine sound effects that loop well and rename the files as specified above.
